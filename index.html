About

Sergiu is a Lecturer in Economics at City University London since September 2013. He received a BSc in Mathematics from Jacobs University Bremen, Germany, and a PhD in Economics from Duke University, along with a Certificate of College Teaching. As an undergraduate, he began with a focus on mathematics and physics, which helped him develop a theoretical aptitude for economics and useful skills such as programming.

His area of concentration is microeconomic theory, with research interests in behavioural economics, decision theory, and dynamic contracting and mechanism design. [1](https://www.citystgeorges.ac.uk/about/people/academics/sergiu-ungureanu)

Research

Sergiu’s research interests include decision theory, behavioural economics, and mechanism design. His work spans microeconomic theory, dynamic contracting, and models of bounded rationality. [1](https://www.citystgeorges.ac.uk/about/people/academics/sergiu-ungureanu)
